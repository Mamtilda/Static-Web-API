About Us

Welcome to Carlton Projects, where craftsmanship meets innovation. Established in 1983, we have been at the forefront of creating bespoke joinery pieces that seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality.

Our mission is to deliver the highest quality joinery items that not only complement their surroundings but also leave a lasting impression. With a team of over twenty skilled artisans, we bring your visions to life.

At Carlton Projects, we are not just a company; we are a community that fosters creativity and growth. We take pride in offering apprenticeships, providing opportunities for the next generation of craftsmen to learn and excel.

Giving back to the community is a cornerstone of our values. We actively engage in charitable activities, including sponsoring events like Pershore Brass, contributing to forest restoration projects, and supporting the Prostate Cancer Research Centre.

Our success is rooted in our team. We have handpicked the finest joiners in the country, each bringing their unique skills and expertise to the table. Whether it's a seasoned craftsman or a young talent, every member is dedicated to achieving excellence.

Join us on this journey of craftsmanship and innovation. Let's create beautiful, functional spaces that stand the test of time.
